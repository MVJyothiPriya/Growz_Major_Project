About Growz

Hello, we help farmers

In Growz, we provide farmers with better agricultural practices through technological innovation and provide them with tools for self-management.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers for agricultural transformation using technology, provide rights for the situation, provide self-management methods, and obtain practical approaches.

Growz Features

  • Business and market rate tracking
  • Region-wise agricultural permissions
  • Fertilizer analysis
  • Knowledge about pests and diseases
  • Permissions from fields
  • Fertilizer analysis and reference
  • Estimates of market demand
  • Central market
  • Arms permissions
  • Seed production
  • Forecast of plant diseases
  • Weather forecasts

How Growz Works

Growz provides all farmers with time and action convenience through a platform. Farmers can inspect their crops well, obtain environmental updates, provide evidence for fertilizer health, know about pests and diseases, and provide market demand information, all in one place.
